## Data Quality Indicators

Services are considered **verified** when they have:
- `fee_schedule` array for fee menu services
- `supporting_documents_detail` with complete document structure
- `conditional_requirements` for certificate-type services
- `client_steps` with proper step-by-step process (with lettered sub-steps for complex services)
- `fees` array with required/optional distinction
- `structured_fees` for variable rate services (RPT, Community Tax)
- `agency_action` field in client steps where applicable
- `website` field for online services
- Detailed `processing_time` with variable conditions noted
- Basic requirement structure for simple services
